About Adopt A Granny

Adopt a Granny is a project for the community that seeks to connect different generations of people.


In the hustle and bustle of modern life, it's evident that many individuals lead exceptionally busy lives. Whether it's the demands of work, study or the necessity to relocate to diverse locations, these circumstances often result in physical distances from loved ones. This phenomenon leaves the younger generation without the comforting presence of parents and grandparents, while simultaneously affecting the elderly who find themselves without the cherished company of their families. The separation can lead to a sense of disconnection and yearning for familial bonds, emphasizing the importance of finding innovative ways to bridge these gaps and create meaningful connections.

This project aim to facilitate these connections and foster a sense of togetherness.

A Guide on navigating the platform

Why join Adopt a Granny

Welcome to Adopt a Granny! We are excited for you to join the community and find new connections.

To get started just create a profile that shows others who you are. It helps to be as precise as possible in order to show who you are. Please also line out what you are looking for. This will help others determine whether you may be a good fit.

The platform works both ways. Anyone can create a profile and upload photos to be found by others who are interested. At the same time you are invited to browse profiles of community members you would be interested in getting to know. If you found someone, just reach out by sending a private message to their inbox.
